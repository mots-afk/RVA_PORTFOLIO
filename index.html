Artist Statement

Seeing, Translating, and Reimagining

This portfolio presents the works I created in Reading Visual Arts as I learned to observe, organize, and communicate ideas through images. Each module challenged me to look more carefully at line, shape, color, value, rhythm, balance, and hierarchy, and to understand how these visual elements affect meaning. From observational drawing and abstract composition to pattern design, poster making, infographic layout, and meme creation, every output pushed me to use both creativity and discipline in different ways.

What I value most in this process is how each artwork became a record of learning. Some pieces required patience and realism, while others demanded experimentation and simplification. Through these activities, I gained a stronger appreciation for visual communication not only as decoration, but as a way of presenting information, emotion, humor, and identity. This collection reflects my development throughout the course and serves as a summary of the skills, ideas, and artistic choices I explored over the semester.
